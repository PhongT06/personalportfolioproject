About Me


I am currently residing in Houston, TX with my beloved wife, our daughter, and our two dogs. I have embraced the warmth and diversity of this vibrant city for over seven years. My journey began in Alabama, where, fresh out of the Radiologic Technology program, I was swiftly cross-trained into a different modality. After earning my Computed Tomography license, Texas became our new home.
